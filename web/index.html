Two fees apply on top of the rate: a 20bp swap fee charged inside the swap/hook, and a flat service fee of 0.05 USDS + 0.10 USDS charged by the contract. The keeper runs every ~2 minutes and executes all pending swaps in one batch, and that fixed 0.10 USDS is split across every swap in the batch — so the more people swapping alongside you, the less each pays. The figure above is the worst case: you're the only swap in the batch.

Changed your mind? Full refund available

Your USDS sits untouched at your deposit address until the batch runs. If you want it back beforehand, call refund() on the ForwarderMinter contract — it returns the full amount with no fee deducted.

How to get a refund
  • 1
    Go to the ForwarderMinter contract on Basescan (link below) and open the Write Contract tab.
  • 2
    Connect your wallet using the "Connect to Web3" button on Basescan.
  • 3
    Find the refund function and enter your wallet address as the user parameter.
  • 4
    Submit the transaction. Your full USDS balance is returned immediately to your wallet.
